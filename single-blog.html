The Art of Layering: How to Create Your Unique Scent

Layering perfumes is a sophisticated way to express your individuality. By combining different olfactory profiles, you can create a custom fragrance that evolves throughout the day, revealing new facets of your personality with every hour.

Start with a heavier base note, such as a deep musk or a rich oud from our I Rasa collection. These scents have larger molecules and will last longer on your skin, providing a solid foundation for lighter notes. Wait a few minutes for the base to settle before adding your second layer.

For the top layer, choose something contrasting or complementary. A bright floral like Jasmine Bloom or a refreshing Citrus Mist can add a sparkling dimension to a deep, woody base. The key is to experiment and find the balance that resonates with your mood and the occasion.

"A perfume is like a piece of clothing, a message, a way of presenting oneself, a costume that differs according to the woman who wears it." - This philosophy drives every creation at I Rasa Perfumes.

Our master perfumers recommend applying fragrance to pulse points�your wrists, neck, and behind the ears�where the skin is warmest. This warmth helps to diffuse the scent naturally. Remember not to rub your wrists together, as this can break down the delicate scent molecules.
