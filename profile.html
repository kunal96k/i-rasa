Shipping, Returns & Refund policies.
What is the typical shipping duration? Domestic orders are usually processed within 24 hours and delivered within 3-5 business days.

Are the perfumes long-lasting? Yes, I Rasa fragrances are highly concentrated (Eau de Parfum / Extrait) and typically last 8-12 hours on skin.
